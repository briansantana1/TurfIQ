Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 12, 2026

Introduction

TurfIQ ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address (if you choose to create an account)
  • Lawn Zone Data: Measurements, coordinates, and zone names you create
  • Product Calculations: Your saved calculation history and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: GPS coordinates when you use the map measurement feature (only when actively using the feature)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting
  • Usage Data: Features used and app performance metrics

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Save your lawn zones and calculation history locally on your device
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when you contact us

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your lawn zones and calculations are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not currently sync your data to cloud servers
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share specific information
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in app operations (e.g., analytics), bound by confidentiality agreements

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Expo: For app development and deployment
  • React Native Maps: For satellite map display
  • AsyncStorage: For local data storage

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt-Out: Disable location services in your device settings
  • Correct: Update or correct your information within the app

Children's Privacy

TurfIQ is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, for significant changes, providing in-app notification.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: info.turfiq@yahoo.com

Data Retention

  • Lawn Zones: Retained until you delete them or uninstall the app
  • Calculation History: Retained until you clear history or uninstall the app
  • Device Data: Retained for up to 90 days for troubleshooting purposes

International Users

TurfIQ is operated from the United States. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information we collect will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion of personal information.
